In 1998, Beatrice Bakery opened its first location on a quiet street corner in the heart of New York City's West Village. From its inception, Beatrice Bakery has been cherished for its classic American baked goods, vintage decor and warm, inviting atmosphere. In 2015, Beatrice Bakery's original owner passed her oven mitts to Luis Cordero and Lily Li. Together, they have thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its first West Village shop to locations worldwide. Today, Beatrice Bakery can be found in New York City, Los Angeles, Chicago, Honolulu, Mexico City, Seoul, Tokyo, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Beirut, Kuwait City, Rome, Cologne. In addition to our current locations, Beatrice Bakery is continuing to franchise internationally and will soon open in Saudi Arabia, Ecuador and Brazil. We are proud to celebrate 19 years ever since the first opening of our bakery.

We are committed to making every pastry from scratch, using fresh, natural, and local ingredients. At Beatrice's Bakery, each batch is carefully prepared, providing top quality sweets and pastries with no shortcuts and our open kitchen concept allows visitors to watch our staff at work. Whether piping away on a tray of freshly baked cupcakes, or mixing up a homemade batch of cookies, the shop is both a delight for the young and old. We also have gluten free options on our menu! So whether a birthday, a dinner party, a wedding or baby shower, let us help cater your next special event!
